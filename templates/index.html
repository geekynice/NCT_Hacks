About Us

FoodNet is on a mission to reduce food waste and make a positive impact on the environment. We connect individuals and businesses with surplus food to those in need, creating a sustainable solution for our community.

Join us in our journey to build a world where no good food goes to waste, and everyone has access to nutritious meals. Together, we can make a difference.

Our Vision

At FoodNet, our vision is to create a world where no one goes to bed hungry, and food waste is minimized. We strive to build a community that cares for the environment and ensures that every person has access to nutritious food.

Join us in making a positive impact on the planet and fostering a culture of sharing and compassion.
